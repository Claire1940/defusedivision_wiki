<Section icon={Target} title="How to Play Your First Matches">

Your first matches should feel simple. Learn the map shape, keep your crosshair at a stable height, and move with the team. If you try to outplay everyone before learning the rhythm, you will usually lose more rounds than you learn from.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join and observe">
    Watch how the lobby splits teams, where players gather, and how the first rotation starts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Pick one role each round">
    Decide whether you are helping attack or defend, then stay committed instead of wandering.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear one lane at a time">
    Check corners, use cover, and avoid sprinting through open space without support.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trade and reset">
    If a teammate falls, take the fight together, then reset your position for the next contact.
  </Step>
</Steps>
