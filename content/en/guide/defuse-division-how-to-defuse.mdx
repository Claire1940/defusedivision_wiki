<Section icon={Shield} title="Plant, Defuse, and Trade Timing">

Timing is where most rounds are won or lost. A strong player does not just aim well; they understand when to peek, when to wait, and when to start the defuse. If the bomb is live, every second should be spent forcing the enemy to make a worse decision than you.

Use the round in phases. Early contact is about locating pressure. Mid-round is about controlling the best space. Late-round is about denying the enemy a clean finish.

  <Step num="1" title="Read the round state">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Win the nearest duel or trade">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cover the defuse path">

  <Step num="4" title="Commit with confidence">
