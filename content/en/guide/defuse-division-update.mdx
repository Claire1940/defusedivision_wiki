<Section icon={Shield} title="Map Control and Defensive Setups">

Holding a site successfully requires proper positioning and crossfires. Defenders must avoid clumped positions where a single flashbang or grenade can disable multiple players. Spread out across the map to gather early info and rotate quickly when the attackers commit to a push.

Understanding common choke points allows you to place utility effectively. Smokes should be thrown to block choke points, forcing attackers to push through blind or delay their execution. Keep track of the round timer, as a delayed attack benefits the defending team.

| Position | Primary Duty | Common Utility Used |
| **A Site Anchor** | Holds A site solo during rotations | Smoke grenade and incendiary |
| **B Site Anchor** | Holds B site solo during rotations | Flashbang and smoke grenade |
| **Mid Player** | Gathers early info and rotates fast | HE grenade and flashbang |
