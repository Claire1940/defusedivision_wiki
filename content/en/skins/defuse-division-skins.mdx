<Section icon={Target} title="Best Ways to Unlock Skins">

For a live Roblox game, cosmetics can arrive through events, giveaways, update rewards, or future content drops. Your job is to separate confirmed unlock paths from community speculation.

  <Step num="1" title="Check the current cosmetic pool">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify the source of the reward">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Claim or complete the requirement">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Record the item details">
